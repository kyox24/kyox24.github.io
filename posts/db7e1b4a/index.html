向量x\bold xx的第j个元素用xjx_jxj​表示，同样向量w\bold ww的第j个元素用wjw_jwj​表示，即：

x=(x1⋮xd),w=(w1⋮wd)\bold x = \begin{pmatrix} x_1 \\ \vdots \\ x_d \end{pmatrix} , \bold w = \begin{pmatrix} w_1 \\ \vdots \\ w_d \end{pmatrix} x=⎝⎜⎛​x1​⋮xd​​⎠⎟⎞​,w=⎝⎜⎛​w1​⋮wd​​⎠⎟⎞​

则矩阵乘积为：

AB=(∑i=1ma1ib1i∑i=1ma1ib2i⋯∑i=1ma1ibni⋮⋮⋱⋮∑i=1malib1i∑i=1malib2i⋯∑i=1malibni)l×n=(a1Tb1a1Tb2⋯a1Tbn⋮⋮⋱⋮alTb1alTb2⋯alTbn)l×n\begin{aligned} AB &= \begin{pmatrix} \sum_{i=1}^ma_{1i}b_{1i}& \sum_{i=1}^ma_{1i}b_{2i}& \cdots& \sum_{i=1}^ma_{1i}b_{ni} \\ \vdots & \vdots& \ddots & \vdots \\ \sum_{i=1}^ma_{li}b_{1i}& \sum_{i=1}^ma_{li}b_{2i}& \cdots& \sum_{i=1}^ma_{li}b_{ni} \end{pmatrix}_{l \times n} \\ \\ &= \begin{pmatrix} {\bold {a}_1}^T\bold b_1& {\bold {a}_1}^T\bold b_2& \cdots & {\bold {a}_1}^T\bold b_n\\ \vdots & \vdots& \ddots & \vdots \\ {\bold {a}_l}^T\bold b_1& {\bold {a}_l}^T\bold b_2& \cdots & {\bold {a}_l}^T\bold b_n \end{pmatrix}_{l \times n} \end{aligned} AB​=⎝⎜⎛​∑i=1m​a1i​b1i​⋮∑i=1m​ali​b1i​​∑i=1m​a1i​b2i​⋮∑i=1m​ali​b2i​​⋯⋱⋯​∑i=1m​a1i​bni​⋮∑i=1m​ali​bni​​⎠⎟⎞​l×n​=⎝⎜⎛​a1​Tb1​⋮al​Tb1​​a1​Tb2​⋮al​Tb2​​⋯⋱⋯​a1​Tbn​⋮al​Tbn​​⎠⎟⎞​l×n​​

正规矩阵

若矩阵A∈Rn×nA \in \mathbb{R}^{n \times n}A∈Rn×n是正规矩阵，则存在B∈Rn×nB \in \mathbb{R}^{n \times n}B∈Rn×n，使得BA=AB=IBA=AB=IBA=AB=I（III为单位矩阵）

若矩阵A∈Rn×nA \in \mathbb{R}^{n \times n}A∈Rn×n是不是正规矩阵，则存在不是零向量的x\bold xx，使得Ax=0A\bold x=\bold 0Ax=0（0\bold 00为零向量）
